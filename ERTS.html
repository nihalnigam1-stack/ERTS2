About ERTS

Empowering Students through Digital Innovation. Moving to a new city for education is a monumental step, but finding the right support shouldn't be a struggle. ERTS (Everything Required To Students) was born out of a simple observation: students spend too much time searching for essentials and not enough time focused on their goals.

We have built a unified digital ecosystem that bridges the gap between students and their local environment. From verified Hostel & Mess listings to emergency Hospital locations and Library resources, ERTS serves as a digital compass for the modern scholar. Our mission is to simplify the student lifestyle, ensuring that every necessity is just a click away.

ERTS is a student-centric platform that aims to provide a one-stop solution for all student needs. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly, intuitive, and accessible from anywhere. We believe that every student deserves to have access to the resources they need to succeed, and we're committed to making that a reality.
